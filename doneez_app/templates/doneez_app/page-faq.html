Customer FAQ

DoneEZ is a user-friendly platform designed to connect you with reliable and highly-rated service professionals in your area. With DoneEZ, you can effortlessly schedule appointments with trusted experts and enjoy peace of mind knowing that your needs will be taken care of efficiently and effectively. Whether you need a plumber, electrician, or any other service provider, DoneEZ simplifies the process of finding and booking skilled professionals, giving you more time to focus on what matters most.

DoneEZ is your ultimate destination for all types of services, no matter how big or small the job may be. From auto mechanics and handymen to house cleaners, tutors, caterers, doctors, lawyers, and everything in between, our platform offers a comprehensive range of services that cater to your every need. Whether you need someone to run a simple errand for you or require a team of professionals to handle a complex project, DoneEZ has got you covered. Our user-friendly website features a complete list of services that you can easily access and book with just a few clicks. So why go anywhere else when you can get all the help you need at DoneEZ?

The benefits of a DoneEZ membership for consumers include access to a network of top-rated service professionals who have been vetted by DoneEZ, the ability to instantly book or request quotes for services, transparent pricing, and the ability to leave reviews and ratings for service professionals. Additionally, DoneEZ provides a satisfaction guarantee for all services booked through the platform, ensuring that consumers are satisfied with the work performed by the service professional.

Joining DoneEZ is completely free! You only pay for the services you book with a participating DoneEZ service professional. There are no hidden fees or upfront costs, and you'll always know the total price of the service before you confirm your booking.

We do not accept payment from service professionals to be listed on our platform. We carefully evaluate each professional's credentials and expertise to ensure they meet our criteria. If they meet our standards, they can join our platform for free.

To become a member of the DoneEZ network, a service professional must meet certain criteria. They must have a minimum of a 4-star rating on at least one of the following online review sites: Yelp or Google Reviews. Additionally, they must have relevant licenses and certifications, adequate insurance coverage, and a demonstrated history of delivering high-quality services to their clients. Our team carefully evaluates each applicant to ensure they meet these criteria before they are approved to join our network.

Membership in the DoneEZ network is not guaranteed for life. Service professionals must maintain a good standing in our network by consistently delivering high-quality services and maintaining a minimum 4-star rating on one of the following review sites: Yelp or Google Reviews. If a service professional's rating falls below 4 stars, their membership may be deactivated. However, they can reactivate their membership once they achieve a 4-star rating or higher. We have this protocol in place to ensure that only the best service professionals are listed on our network and that our customers receive the best possible service.

We want to ensure that each new category meets our high standards for quality and reliability before it is added to our platform. Please stay tuned for updates on our expanding service offerings.

Supplier FAQ

DoneEZ is a B2B platform designed for suppliers to promote and sell their products and services to service professional members. By leveraging our platform, you can showcase your offerings on the members' dashboard, making it easy for them to discover and purchase from you.

As a supplier, selling on the DoneEZ dashboard offers you several benefits. Not only can you sell more parts by showcasing your offerings to our reputable auto service professionals, but you can also ensure that your parts are being installed correctly. This reduces the number of part returns, the need for costly replacements, and the loss of sales due to defective parts. Furthermore, selling on our platform enables you to establish long-term relationships with service professionals and increase your brand visibility in the industry.

If you're interested in being listed as a supplier on our platform, you'll be pleased to know that there are no fees involved. As a supplier, you can list and sell your products on the Pro dashboard for free.

If you're interested in being listed as a supplier on our site, the requirements are straightforward. We partner with suppliers whose products and services can offer value to our service professional members. Additionally, our partner suppliers are requested to provide a discount to our service professional members.
