How it Works

Looking after your car has never been simpler! You can now have your car repaired or serviced by one of our highly-rated auto service professionals. Rather than spending countless hours searching through business listings and review sites, and dealing with the tedious task of obtaining price quotes, all you need to do is submit an auto service request on DoneEZ. We will notify auto service professionals in your area and ask them to provide a price quote for your job. With just a few clicks, you can select the auto service pro you think is the best fit for you, and consider the job done!

1) Post a Service

Select your repair service.

2) Get Competitive Quotes

Browse pros, preview the quotes and select the one you like.

3) Book an Appointment

Tell us when to meet you at your home or office. Or you can bring it to your selected repair shop.

4) Get Your Car Fixed

Enjoy top rated auto repair services. Satisfaction guaranteed! You pay only when your job is done to your satisfaction.
